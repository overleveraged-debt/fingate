Documents Required

To ensure quick processing of your loan application, please keep the following documents ready based on your employment type.

For Salaried Customers

  • Latest 3 months salary slip.
  • Latest Form 16
  • Last 6 months Bank statements
  • ID Proof (Applicant & Co Applicant)
  • Address Proof, Office & Residence (Applicant & Co Applicant)
  • Applicant & Co Applicant Photograph
  • Processing fee cheque.
  • Property Documents (Mother Deed,Khatha,EC, Sanction Plan,Sale Agreements) Etc.

Self Employed Customers

  • Latest 3 Yrs ITR with P/L & B/S
  • Last 1 Year Bank statements (Self & Business)
  • ID Proof (Applicant & Co Applicant)
  • Address Proof,Office & Residence (Applicant & Co Applicant)
  • Applicant & co Applicant Photograph
  • Processing fee cheque.
  • Property Documents (Mother Deed,Khatha,EC, Sanction Plan,Sale Agreements) Etc.
  • Business Profile & Registration Certificate

Additional Docs for Co./Firms

  • Latest 3 Years ITR with P/L & B/S of the company.
  • Last 1 Year Bank statements of the company
  • MOA/AOA /Partnership Deed of the firm.
  • Registration /Incorporation Certificate
  • Vat return for latest 12 months
  • Loan repayment track record if any.
